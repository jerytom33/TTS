## Step-by-Step Guide

### Creating a Project with Voice Preview

1. **Go to Dashboard**
   - Click "New Project" button

2. **Enter Project Details**
   - Project Name: "My Marketing Video"
   - Description: "30-second promotional video"

3. **Enter Your Text**
   - Paste or type the text you want as voiceover
   - Example: "Welcome to our amazing product..."

4. **Select Voice**
   - Choose from available voices
   - Click dropdown and select (English/Malayalam available)

5. **Adjust Settings** (Optional)
   - **Speed**: Make voice faster (1.5x) or slower (0.8x)
   - **Pitch**: Make voice higher or lower (1.5x to 0.5x)

6. **Generate Audio**
   - Click "Generate Audio" button
   - Wait for processing (~2-10 seconds)

7. **Preview Voice**
   - Audio player appears with your generated voice
   - Click â–¶ Play to listen
   - Adjust volume if needed

8. **Download or Save**
   - **To keep it**: Click "Save Project"
   - **To download audio**: Click ğŸ“¥ button in player
   - **To regenerate**: Adjust settings and click "Generate Audio" again

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I slow down playback speed?
**A:** Not with the player controls. Instead, adjust "Speed" setting to 0.5x - 2.0x before generating audio.

### Q: How do I download audio?
**A:** In the audio player, click the ğŸ“¥ **Download** button. The MP3 file will save to your Downloads folder.

### Q: What if the audio doesn't play?
**A:** 
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if problem persists

### Q: Can I edit the audio after generation?
**A:** Currently, no. You must regenerate with different settings or select a different voice.

### Q: What audio formats are supported?
**A:** MP3 (recommended), WAV, OGG, AAC, M4A

### Q: Why is audio loading slowly?
**A:** 
- Check internet connection
- First load may be slower
- Try refreshing the page

### Q: Can I use audio on commercial projects?
**A:** Yes! All generated audio is yours to use commercially. No restrictions.

### Q: How do I get more voices?
**A:** Go to Admin Panel to manage available voices or request new languages.
